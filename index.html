What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is a free, open-world action role-playing game that allows the player to control one of four interchangeable characters in a party.


Why should you play Genshin Impact in 2022?

There's an enchanting subsurface region full of yet more secrets to unearth, story quests which feature new and returning characters in fun ways, and possibly the best music in a soundtrack which has delivered non-stop anthems since day one.
